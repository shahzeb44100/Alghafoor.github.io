    Basmati Rice

    Basmati Rice is one of the most popular varieties of the long grain rice. Its seamless quality along with its cost-effective prices make it a preferred choice of rice variety for Moms and many of the cooks worldwide.

    The Basmati Rice is processed through steaming, and offers long, clean, and aromatic grains to the food lovers.

    Also it takes very less time to cook. The steaming process includes passing steam through the paddy grains, then removing of Paddy layers while drying them out.

    Through this process, the rice grain stays white, while its surface becomes harder to give it strength to not get broken.
